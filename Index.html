Profile

Rasyiid Fadhilah Yusuf is a music producer who is famous for his music in the EDM genre. Especially the "4 On The Floor Family" genre or could be called "House Family". He has joined the 1st generation Rannako Entertainment agency and was the co-founder of the agency. Born on 23 December 2004 in Mojokerto, he has entered the music industry with 3 years of experience.
